♡ About Us ♡

Flower Knows is a Chinese cosmetics brand celebrated for its whimsical,
fairy-tale-inspired designs and high-quality makeup. Known for artistic
packaging and imaginative themes, it appeals to beauty enthusiasts and collectors
worldwide. Drawing inspiration from mythical creatures and vintage European aesthetics,
the brand offers products with vibrant pigmentation, smooth textures, and
lasting performance. Popular collections like the Unicorn Series, Angel Collection,
and Rococo Collection showcase its unique, fantasy-inspired charm.
